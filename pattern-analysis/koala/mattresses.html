FAQs about mattresses

Which Koala mattress is best for me?

The Koala Mattress Range has been thoughtfully designed, with mattress options for every kind of sleeper and budget.

Looking for the right back support with the flexibility to adjust the firmness to your liking or to match different body types and body weights? The Koala Mattress might be the way to go. It's Australia's top-rated new mattress for a reason, combining 7cm of Kloudcell® comfort with firmness adjustability to suit your unique sleeping style.

If you want to step up your sleep comfort, take a look at the Koala Plus Mattress. It features a generous 8cm of Cooling Kloudcell® and five targeted high-density support zones designed to provide good support exactly where your body needs it. The Koala Plus offers advanced support & cooling with seasonal and firmness adjustment, packed with top-notch cooling tech and a double-sided topper that you can flip depending on the season, ensuring comfort all year round.

For those who need next-level temperature regulation, the new Koala Polar+ Mattress delivers the ultimate cooling with PolarBands™—enhancing airflow and reducing heat buildup during sleep. As our most advanced cooling mattress,  it offers all the benefits of the Koala Plus Mattress, including advanced support and seasonal adjustability, with even greater cooling performance, making it perfect for hot sleepers.

For those who want to experience life-changing sleep, check out the Koala Luxe Mattress. It’s our plushest option yet, with a 9cm Kloudcell® Comfort Layer infused with copper for superior pressure relief and temperature regulation. It’s designed to offer the contouring comfort of memory foam mattresses with enhanced breathability and support. 

Plus, with seven targeted high-density support zones, it provides the ultimate support for every sleeping position. To top it off, we've added a touch of luxury with a sumptuously soft Australian cashmere and linen-blended boucle for next-level comfort.

Each mattress in our lineup is designed to help you get the best sleep possible. Whether you need adaptable support, advanced cooling, or the plush feel of a medium-firm mattress, Koala has the perfect fit for you.

Still on the fence? Check out our mattress options in our comparison table or take our quiz here.

How do Koala mattresses cater to different sleep positions?

Firmness preferences vary based on sleeping position, which is why we offer different mattresses to suit all needs. Back sleepers often benefit from firmer foam mattresses that promote spinal alignment, while side sleepers may prefer softer options for reducing pressure points around the shoulders and hips. Stomach sleepers, on the other hand, typically require extra support to prevent spinal stress.

The Koala Mattress and Koala Plus Mattress are mattresses designed for versatility, featuring a flippable comfort layer so you can choose between medium to firm support. The Koala Luxe Mattress takes customisation even further, offering a half-and-half comfort layer for couples with different preferences—reducing partner disturbance for a truly restful night.

For those looking for ultimate plushness, the Koala Luxe Mattress is our best mattress for luxurious comfort. Engineered with seven high-density support zones, it delivers superior contouring while reducing pressure points for more restorative sleep.

After nine years of perfecting our Kloudcell® technology, we've achieved the perfect balance of softness and support. The adaptive properties of our memory foam-like material help back and stomach sleepers feel properly supported while providing just the right cushioning for side sleepers. No matter how you sleep, our mattresses ensure a good night’s sleep every night.

Which is the firmest mattress in the range?

If you’re looking for a quality mattress with firm support, the Koala Mattress or the Koala Plus Mattress with Flippable Firmness could be perfect for you. Simply flip the comfort layer to switch between 'medium-firm' and extra firm, tailoring the feel to your exact comfort level.

The Koala Mattress also offers a Partner Preference option, allowing couples to personalise their firmness levels for undisturbed sleep. Plus, our high-density materials provide additional support, helping to maintain structure and durability over time—so you won’t notice sagging after just a few years.

Available in a range of mattress sizes, our designs offer comfort with advanced support technology. Experience the perfect balance of firmness and pressure alleviation without compromising on comfort.

Which is the plushest mattress in the range?

If luxurious plush comfort is what you’re after, check out the Koala Luxe Mattress—our best mattress for ultimate softness and support. Featuring a 9cm Kloudcell® Comfort Layer infused with copper, it provides unparalleled relief while keeping you cool and fresh throughout the night.

Designed to enhance your sleep quality and overall well-being, the Luxe Mattress is packed with seven targeted high-density support zones to cradle your body in all the right places. It delivers the contouring comfort of latex mattresses, without the drawbacks, ensuring a rejuvenating sleep experience.

For even more personalised comfort, pair it with our mattress toppers for extra plushness. Available in sizes up to a super king, this premium mattress blends luxury with innovative support—so you can sleep in unmatched serenity, night after night.

What support technology is used in Koala Mattresses?

Every Koala Mattress is designed with a sophisticated base of ergonomic support zones, ensuring a comfortable mattress that contours to your body’s natural curves while promoting healthier spinal alignment for better sleep quality.

The SE Mattress and Koala Mattress both feature three high-density targeted support zones under the torso, hips, and legs to provide essential reinforcement. For additional comfort, the Koala Plus Mattress enhances this with five targeted zones, offering even more precise support. Our most advanced model, the Koala Luxe Mattress, introduces seven precision-cut zones designed to optimise spinal alignment for all mattress types and sleeping positions.

Unlike pocket-spring mattresses, Koala designs rely on advanced Kloudcell® and foam layers, ensuring superior pressure relief with no springs. Plus, our mattresses deliver the contouring benefits of memory foam and the responsive feel of latex mattresses, making them ideal for sleepers who want the best of both worlds.

With a tiered support system across all our designs, every Koala Mattress adapts to your unique needs—so you can experience comfort, support, and high-quality sleep every night.

Which is the coolest mattress in the range?

If you struggle to stay cool at night, the new Koala Polar+ Mattress is our coolest mattress yet, designed to keep you up to 5°C cooler while you sleep. Featuring PolarBands™ technology, Cooling Kloudcell®, and a breathable, reversible topper, it enhances airflow and draws heat away for a fresher, more comfortable rest.

Unlike traditional foam mattresses, the Koala Polar+ Mattress provides targeted temperature regulation by removing excess body heat. Whether you prefer a medium-firm or a firmer feel, this mattress adapts to your needs—helping you stay cool, enjoy a good night's sleep, and wake up refreshed.

- Single: 188 x 92cm

When shopping for Bed linen and Mattress Protectors, ensure they are compatible with the height of your mattress. Our TENCEL™ Bed Linen and Great Barrier Mattress Protector are also available in 'Deep Fit' sizes, ideal for accommodating the taller Koala Luxe Mattress and ensuring a snug, secure fit.

What is Kloudcell®?

Kloudcell® is what quality sleep is made of. With its superior support, breathability, responsiveness, and durability, Kloudcell® is the closest experience to floating on a cloud that you can have.

Every Koala Mattress uses Kloudcell® technology in the comfort layer, providing the perfect combination of softness and support. The Koala Mattress and Koala Plus Mattress feature Flippable Firmness, allowing you to choose between medium-firm and firm at home. 

For those who love the contouring feel of memory foam but want enhanced breathability, Kloudcell® offers the best of both worlds. The Koala Luxe Mattress takes plushness to the next level with 3cm of Kloudcell® quilting—containing 50% more Kloudcell® than the SE Mattress.

Upgrading from an old mattress? Kloudcell® technology ensures long-lasting comfort and support, making every night’s sleep better than the last. As Australia’s most awarded mattress range with over 50,000 five-star reviews, falling asleep faster and staying asleep longer is as good as a sure thing. Learn more.

What is Zero Disturbance® technology?

Sleeping next to a tosser? Our famous Zero Disturbance® technology is featured in all mattress types designed to absorb movement, ensuring you stay undisturbed when your partner tosses and turns. 

Whether you prefer a medium firm mattress or something plusher, Koala mattresses provide tailored support for different sleep styles and body weight, so both you and your partner get the best mattress experience—without compromise.

What is a mattress in a box? How long does it take for a Koala Mattress to expand?

A Koala quality mattress is compressed, rolled, and vacuum-sealed into a compact box for quick and easy delivery straight to your door. Whether you need a super king or a more compact size, we offer a different mattress for every sleeper.

Made with high-density foam, Koala mattresses take around 3-5 days to fully expand to their correct shape and feel. However, you can sleep on it right away—it only gets better over time! If you need extra firm support, rest assured that your mattress will quickly adapt to provide the perfect balance of comfort and durability.

What is your approach to sustainability?

At Koala, we're committed to making a positive impact on the environment through thoughtful actions and genuine sustainability efforts.

Koala Mattresses feature foams certified by CertiPUR-US®, meaning they are made without ozone depleters and heavy metals, whilst promoting indoor air quality through low Volatile Organic Compounds (VOCs). We also integrate materials like carbon-zero TENCEL™, derived from sustainable wood sources, and recycled fibres, with the aim to reduce our footprint and promote the use of sustainable resources.

In addition to choosing thoughtful materials, we actively support environmental conservation by partnering with WWF and participating in the 1% for the Planet initiative. This means a portion of every mattress sale is invested in projects that aim to protect our planet's natural habitats and biodiversity.

Our approach to sustainability is about taking concrete steps to minimise our environmental impact, while supporting global efforts to preserve the planet for future generations.

What is CertiPUR-US®?

Koala Mattresses only use foam that has been CertiPUR-US® certified, meaning it has been tested to meet CertiPUR-US®' rigorous standards for emissions, content, performance, and durability. The foam in our mattresses is made without harmful phthalates, and they are made without the use of ozone-depleting substances including methylene chloride and other CFCs. Koala Mattress foam has also been tested to have low VOC (Volatile Organic Compound) emissions for indoor air quality (less than 0.5 parts per million).

What type of bed frame do you recommend?

Choosing the right mattress is only part of the equation—your bed frame matters too. As long as your base provides adequate support and ventilation, it will work with any Koala Mattress, including our memory foam mattresses. If you're using a slat base, keep the slats no more than 7.5cm apart to ensure proper mattress support.

While posture slats can be used, they may make the mattress feel softer than intended. For the best comfort—especially if you prefer extra firm support—we recommend straight (flat) slats.

We don’t suggest placing your mattress directly on the floor without ventilation, as this can cause moisture build-up. Similarly, adjustable beds aren't ideal, as bending or folding the mattress may damage the core or supportive layers.

If you’re upgrading your bedroom furniture, check out our bed bases—they're beautiful, strong, and easy to assemble!
